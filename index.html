Paradise Cove

Boutique Hotel Mauritius

LOCATION

Situated on the north coast of Mauritius at Anse la Raie, the hotel enjoys an exclusive site with its natural cove, its love nests and its peninsula. The lively village of Grand Baie is situated 10 minutes away by car and the authentic fishermen’s village of Cap Malheureux only 2.5 km away.

The Team

At Paradise Cove Boutique Hotel, there are 184 Family Members, including 7 presents from the opening of the hotel, delighted to welcome you for an unforgettable stay. They always offer a personalized and caring service with a smile, reflecting thus the genuine Mauritian hospitality.

Guillaume Tyack - General Manager

Ambitious and dynamic, Guillaume Tyack has been in the hotel business for more than 10 years. Guillaume Tyack holds a Diploma in Marketing (from the Institute of Marketing Management – South Africa); he has worked for big hotel groups in Mauritius, Paris and the Maldives. After beginning in events and sales, he joined the teams of the hotel operations where he thrives since. He joined the Attitude Hotels group in January 2013 and is now the General Manager of two hotels of the group: Zilwa Attitude since 2016 and Paradise Cove Boutique Hotel since 2018.

AWARDS
